Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MyMate ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile and web application (the "App" or "Service").

Information We Collect

  • Account information: If you sign in with a third-party provider (such as Google), we receive basic profile information like your name, email address, and a unique account identifier.
  • Conversation data: Messages you send and receive within the App, used to provide and improve the chat experience.
  • Usage data: General information about how you interact with the App, such as feature usage and session activity.
  • Device information: Basic technical information such as device type, operating system, and app version, used for diagnostics and compatibility.

How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the App's core chat functionality.
  • To keep your conversation history associated with your account across sessions and devices.
  • To improve, personalize, and troubleshoot the Service.
  • To communicate with you about support requests you initiate.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to power parts of the App, including AI language providers for generating responses and authentication providers (such as Google) for optional sign-in. These providers process data only as needed to provide their service to us and are bound by their own privacy and security practices.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with service providers as necessary to operate the App, or where required by law.

Data Retention

We retain conversation and account data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Contact Us below).

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your region), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the "Last updated" date at the top of this page whenever changes are made.

Contact Us
